WARCOP Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 13, 2026

WARCOP (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") is committed to protecting users' personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act of the Republic of Korea. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

Article 1. Data Collected and Collection Methods

1. Data Collected

The Company collects the following personal information to provide its services:

CategoryData Items

2. Collection Methods

  • Direct input by users during registration and service usage
  • Automatic collection through Google social login
  • Information automatically generated during service usage

Article 2. Purpose of Collection and Use

The Company uses collected personal information only for the following purposes:

Service provisionMember identification & authentication, menu order processing, delivery service execution, payment confirmation
Delivery executionDelivery address confirmation, rider assignment, real-time location tracking, delivery completion notification
AI quality improvementChatbot response accuracy analysis, consultation history management, statistical analysis for service improvement

Marketing & notificationsEvent & discount information, push notification delivery (with separate consent)
Legal obligationsCompliance with related laws including the E-Commerce Act and Communications Privacy Act

Article 6. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  1. Request to access personal information
  2. Request to correct or delete personal information
  3. Request to suspend processing of personal information
  4. Account deletion (deletion of personal information)

How to Exercise

  • Directly through the [Profile > Settings > Privacy Management] menu in the app
  • Written request via email (warcop.app@gmail.com)
  • Processed within 10 days of receiving the request, and the result will be notified.

Article 7. Data Destruction

1. Destruction Procedure

Personal information that has fulfilled its collection purpose is transferred to a separate database and destroyed after being stored for a specified period in accordance with internal policies and relevant laws.

2. Destruction Methods

  • Electronic files: Permanently deleted using methods that prevent recovery
  • Paper documents: Shredded or incinerated

Article 8. Data Security Measures

The Company takes the following measures to ensure the security of personal information:

  1. Administrative measures: Minimizing personnel handling personal data, conducting regular training
  2. Technical measures: Data encryption (TLS 1.2 or higher), access rights management, security program operation
  3. Physical measures: Server room access control (using cloud services)

Article 9. Use of Location Information

  1. The Company collects and uses user location information to provide delivery services.
  2. Location information is collected only during delivery orders and deleted immediately after delivery completion.
  3. Users may refuse to provide location information through app or device settings. In this case, use of delivery services may be restricted.

Article 10. AI Chatbot Data Processing

  1. Conversation content entered when using the AI chatbot is processed for the purpose of generating responses and improving service quality.
  2. Conversation logs are stored for 90 days and then automatically deleted.
  3. Conversation content may be transmitted to an external AI service (OpenAI) for response generation. At this time, personally identifiable information is anonymized.
  4. Users may request deletion of conversation history at any time.

Article 11. Cookies

The Company may use cookies for user convenience. Users may refuse cookie collection through device settings.
